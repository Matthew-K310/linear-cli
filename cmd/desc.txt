
linear-cli is a powerful command-line interface tool designed to bring the core functionality of Linear.app directly to your terminal. It allows you to manage your Linear projects, issues, and workflows without leaving the command line environment. This offers a fast, efficient, and scriptable way to interact with your Linear workspace, making it ideal for developers and power users who prefer a terminal-centric workflow. With linear-cli, you can typically perform actions such as: 1. Creating new issues 2. Viewing existing issues and their details 3. Updating issue status, assignees, and other properties 4. Listing projects and teams 5. Searching for specific issues By integrating Linear management into your terminal, linear-cli facilitates seamless transitions between coding, terminal commands, and project tracking, enhancing productivity and enabling automation of routine tasks.
